# AI Intelligence Expansion Plan

## Objective
Integrate AI to automate POS commands and inventory data entry.

## Component 1: POS AI Assistant
- **Endpoint**: `api/pos_ai_helper.php`
- **UI**: Search bar in `pos.php` labeled "AI Command".
- **Functionality**: Parse text like "Add 3 CPUs for James" into cart actions.

## Component 2: Inventory AI Copywriter
- **Endpoint**: `api/inventory_ai_helper.php`
- **UI**: "Suggest" button in `inventory/add.php`.
- **Functionality**: Generate description/price from product name.
